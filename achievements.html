e-Yantra Robotics Competition

2017-18

Contribute to the team's first place finish in the All India e-yantra competition in the track 'Collector Bot'.The theme of this competition was constructing two robots called 'Collector Bot'and 'Truck' marked with Aruco markers, that perform fruit collection. I worked on motion planning, path planning, OpenCV image processing, PID algorithm in Python using the software V-REP, and microcontroller programming for the robots. The objective was to construct a robot that would collect and deposit fruits, avoiding obstacles using an efficient algorithm in the shortest possible time.

Interthrone- Startup Competition

2017

Our startup 'e-Nirogya' won the Interthrone competition held by Reliance Unlimit and were funded Rs 300k. Our mission was to eradicate the lack of health care facilities in rural India by creating a chain of primary health care units that offer high quality, affordable outpatient care in low income, densely populated regions, where health care delivery by the state is sparse.

Transform Maharashtra

2016

Achieved third place in the people's choice award in the state of Maharashtra for our presentation on the plight of the 'Urban Poor'. Presented our proposal in the theme ’Urban Poor’ by providing solutions to the lack of education and healthcare in the urban poor. The team won a combined prize money of Rs 20k.
